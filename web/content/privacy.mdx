# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: February 14, 2026_

Orca ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Orca mobile application (the "App"). By using Orca, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide and improve Orca's language learning experience.

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account or use the App, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Name**
- **Email address**
- **Gender**
- **Birthday** (used to verify age eligibility and personalize learning)
- **Profile image**
- **Native language**
- **Learning language**

### 1.2 Voice and Speech Data

Orca uses **speech recognition** to help you practice pronunciation and speaking.

- Voice input is captured and processed in real time to evaluate your pronunciation and provide feedback.
- Speech recognition is performed using on-device or platform-provided technologies where possible.
- Voice data may also be transmitted to our AI systems for the purpose of powering real-time conversational lessons with the Orca AI teacher.
- We do **not** store raw voice recordings for general use, except where explicitly required for AI-assisted lesson evaluation or technical troubleshooting.
- In **audio study rooms** (the collaborative voice feature), your microphone audio is transmitted in real time to other participants in the room. We do not record or retain room audio after sessions end.

### 1.3 Camera and Screen Data

Orca uses the camera and screen recording capabilities of your device for certain interactive pronunciation game features.

- Camera access is used to support pronunciation games that incorporate visual feedback during gameplay.
- Screen recording may be initiated during specific in-app game sessions to capture gameplay for evaluation purposes.
- This data is used only within the context of the specific game feature and is not stored or shared beyond what is necessary for that feature to function.
- You will be prompted for explicit permission before camera or screen recording access is used.

### 1.4 User-Generated Content

Orca allows users to create and share content within the community. This includes:

- **Posts**: Text and media you publish on the Orca social feed.
- **Comments**: Replies you leave on other users' posts.
- **Reactions**: Emoji or other reactions you apply to posts.

This content is stored on our servers and is visible to other users within the App. Please do not share personally sensitive information in posts or comments.

### 1.5 Audio Room Activity

Orca includes a collaborative audio room feature (similar to live audio communities) where users can join rooms to study together by voice.

- When you join a room, your microphone audio is broadcast in real time to other room participants.
- Room membership (i.e., who is present in a room) may be visible to other users.
- We do not record or retain audio from study rooms after a session concludes.

### 1.6 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect limited technical information, such as:

- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Language preferences
- Anonymous usage data (e.g., feature usage, crashes)

This data is used strictly to improve performance, stability, and user experience.

### 1.7 Subscription and Purchase Information

If you subscribe to Orca+, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not directly collect or store your payment card details. We may receive confirmation of your subscription status and transaction identifiers from the platform provider and from RevenueCat, our subscription management service.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your Orca account
- Personalize your language learning experience
- Match you with other students learning the same language
- Provide speaking, listening, and reading exercises
- Evaluate pronunciation and fluency via AI-powered lessons and speech recognition
- Operate the Orca AI teacher for real-time conversational lessons
- Power community features including posts, comments, reactions, and audio study rooms
- Process and manage Orca+ subscription access
- Communicate important updates or account-related messages
- Improve and optimize the App's features and performance

We do **not** sell your personal data.

---

## 3. Text-to-Speech, Speech Recognition, AI Lessons, and Audio

Orca uses the following technologies:

- **Speech Recognition** (via Expo / device APIs) to analyze spoken phrases during pronunciation exercises and games
- **AI Voice Agent (Orca Teacher)**: An AI-powered conversational teacher communicates with you by voice during lessons. Voice input is transmitted to our AI systems in real time for processing and response generation.
- **Offline on-device Text-to-Speech (Piper TTS)** to generate natural voice lessons
- **Background music** to enhance the learning experience
- **Live audio transmission** for collaborative study rooms (audio is transmitted to room participants in real time and is not recorded)

### Key Privacy Notes

- Text-to-speech is generated **on-device** and does not require sending your data to external servers.
- Background audio does not collect or process personal data.
- Speech recognition and AI lesson audio are used only for learning features and not for identification purposes.
- AI lesson voice sessions may be processed by third-party AI providers under strict data protection agreements.

## 4. Camera and Screen Recording

Orca requests access to your camera and, on supported platforms, screen recording capabilities for specific interactive pronunciation game features.

- Camera and screen data are used only during active gameplay sessions.
- This data is not continuously monitored, stored beyond the session, or used for any purpose other than the specific feature being used.
- You may revoke camera permissions at any time through your device settings, though doing so will disable certain game features.

## 6. Audio Study Rooms

The audio room feature allows users to join live, voice-based study sessions with other learners.

- Your voice is transmitted in real time to other participants when your microphone is active.
- Room membership (who has joined a room) is visible to other users in the App.
- Audio sessions are **not recorded or stored** after a session ends.
- You may mute yourself at any time during a session.
