# Restore Purchases

_Last updated: February 14, 2026_

If you previously subscribed to **echo+** and your subscription is no longer active in the app — for example after reinstalling the app, switching devices, or logging into a new account — you can restore your purchase without being charged again.

---

## How to Restore Your Purchase

1. Open the echo app and navigate to **Settings** or the **echo+** subscription screen.
2. Tap **Restore Purchases**.
3. echo will check your Apple App Store or Google Play account for any active or prior echo+ subscriptions.
4. If a valid subscription is found, your echo+ access will be restored immediately.

---

## When Should You Restore?

You may need to restore your purchase if:

- You reinstalled echo on the same or a different device
- You signed in with a different account and your echo+ access is missing
- You upgraded or reset your phone
- echo+ features appear locked despite a prior purchase

---

## Requirements

- You must be signed in to the **same Apple ID or Google account** that was used to make the original purchase.
- Your echo+ subscription must still be **active and not expired** at the time of the restore.
- An internet connection is required to verify your subscription status.

---

## Subscription Status After Restore

| Situation                           | Result                                      |
| ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Active subscription on same account | ✅ Access restored immediately              |
| Expired subscription                | ❌ No access — subscription must be renewed |
| Purchased on a different account    | ❌ Must sign in with the original account   |
| Cancelled but within billing period | ✅ Access restored until period ends        |

---

## Troubleshooting

**Restore shows no active subscription**
Make sure you are signed in to the Apple ID or Google account that was originally used to purchase echo+. echo cannot transfer purchases between accounts.

**I cancelled but my period hasn't ended**
echo+ access continues until the end of your paid billing period. Tap Restore Purchases to re-sync your status.

**I switched from iOS to Android (or vice versa)**
Subscriptions made through the Apple App Store are only valid on iOS, and subscriptions made through Google Play are only valid on Android. Cross-platform transfers are not supported by Apple or Google. You would need to subscribe again on your new platform.

**The restore button is not working**
Try signing out of your Apple ID or Google account and signing back in, then attempt the restore again. If the issue persists, contact us at [hi@ahmedbna.com](mailto:hi@ahmedbna.com).

---

## Refund Policy

All echo+ purchases are **non-refundable**. Restoring a purchase does not constitute a new charge. If you believe you were charged in error, please contact Apple or Google directly through your device's App Store or Play Store account settings, as all billing is handled by those platforms.
